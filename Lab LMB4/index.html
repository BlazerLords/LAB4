About Team

Sea Wolf is not just a football team, it is a symbol of resilience and courage embodied in every player. Founded in 2023 in the port city of Odessa, the team quickly won the love and respect of its fans. Playing with heart and passion, 'Sea Wolf' continues to inspire new generations to achieve success on and off the football field.

Unique

Sea Wolf brings its own personality to the field, creating a unique team dynamic. ‘Sea Wolf’ is a team that strives for excellence in every match, despite all the difficulties. They inspire their fans with their passion for the game and their desire to win.

Awards

Bronze medalist of the USSR 1974
Silver medalist of the Ukrainian 1995
